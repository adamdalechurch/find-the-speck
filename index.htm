Find the Speck...

Objective:

Find and click on the elusive speck, or accurately declare when there is no speck visible, all before your flashlight's battery life runs out.

How to Play:

  • Find the Speck: Move your cursor around the screen to use your flashlight. Click on the speck when you find it.
  • Speck Visibility: Sometimes the speck may disappear. Stay alert and make a decision based on its visibility.
  • Battery Life: Each successful click on the speck increases your flashlight's battery life. Incorrect clicks decrease it.
  • Declaring No Speck: If you believe there is no speck, click the 'There is No Speck' button. Correct declarations increase your battery life, while incorrect ones decrease it.
  • Game Over: The game ends when your flashlight's battery runs out. Try to achieve a high score before this happens!
  • Restarting the Game: You can restart the game anytime to try and improve your score.

Tips for Success:

  • Keep your cursor moving and stay vigilant to spot the speck or its absence quickly.
  • Balance between searching for the speck and conserving battery life by making careful decisions.
  • Pay close attention to the changing visibility of the speck and respond quickly.

Good luck, and have fun playing "Find the Speck"!
